LOCUS Data

The telematics data utilized in this analysis are sourced from a sample of connected vehicles and connected navigation mobile applications. These data provide detailed information regarding trip origins and destinations, traffic volumes, speeds, and congestion, aggregated to zones and roadway segments. While the dataset is granular, it represents a sample of the total trip population, typically encompassing at least twenty-five to thirty percent (25–30%) of all trips. This sample is generally considered representative of overall travel patterns.

  1. Connected navigation applications constitute approximately eighty to ninety percent (80–90%) of the sample and are widely adopted across the traveling population.
  2. Connected vehicles comprise the remaining ten to twenty percent (10–20%) and primarily include newer model vehicles, which may introduce a slight bias toward higher-income travelers.

Origin-Destination (OD) Analysis

Parking lot geofencing boundaries have been established based on the official NRG Stadium parking lot map. Trips identified as traveling to or from NRG are determined using these geofencing boundaries. Such trips are not necessarily associated with event visitors and may include other non-event-related travel.

Congestion Analysis

Congestion levels are assessed using the ratio of Average Speed to Posted Speed Limit to identify roadway bottlenecks and congestion severity. Please note that posted speed limit data may not be fully accurate, as certain records could be outdated at the time of analysis.
